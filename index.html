About Dove Solutions

Dove Solutions provides reliable IT support and technical consulting for homes and small businesses in Edmonton and surrounding areas. Built on hands-on experience in technical support, troubleshooting, and network diagnostics, we focus on solving real technology problems with practical, effective solutions.

Our approach is straightforward: communicate clearly, diagnose issues efficiently, and recommend only what is truly needed. We understand how disruptive computer, printer, and network issues can be, and we work to resolve them without unnecessary complexity or upselling.

From device configuration to onsite investigations and network support, Dove Solutions is committed to helping clients reduce downtime, improve reliability, and feel confident in their technology.
